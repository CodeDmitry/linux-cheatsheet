1. To insert N blank lines, enter the number of lines, then press shift-O, then press escape.
2. To delete N lines, enter the number, then press dd.
3. To go to the end of the file, then press shift-G.
4. To go to the start of the file, type gg.
5. To go to Nth line, enter the line number to jump to, then press shift-G.
